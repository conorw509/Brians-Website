About the trip

In September, Brian and Rita will be taking part in the annual CWUHA humanitarian aid trip. The CWUHA is a response to the plight of vulnerable children in countries Worldwide from trade unionists to family and friends who work in Britain and Ireland’s postal and telecom industries. Over the past number of years the CWUHA has delivered thousands of tonnes of aid directly to hundreds of orphanages, foster and adoption centres, rundown hospitals and vulnerable families. This year they will be travelling to Moldova in Eastern Europe with Aid. An Post supply the truck and the diesel. The Union cover the cost of the ferries and the accommodation, allowing all money raised to go to the children. The trip will take place in September 2017 and will take two weeks. The two Irish vehicles will meet with 8 other vehicles in the UK before departing to mainland Europe. The two An Post employees will be driving to Moldova and need to raise as much money as possible.
